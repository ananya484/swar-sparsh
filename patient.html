Website Flow and Steps

Our platform is designed to provide a seamless experience for managing your therapy sessions and resources. Here's how you can navigate the website:

  1. Start by filling out your profile to provide us with essential information that helps tailor the therapy sessions to your needs.
  2. Access various therapy games from the dashboard to improve your speech and communication skills.
  3. Join video calls with your therapist for live, interactive sessions.
  4. Use the chatbot feature to have a conversation or get instant support whenever needed.
  5. Interact with partners and explore additional resources through the partner interaction section.
  6. Upload documents and answer questionnaires for your therapist to review.
  7. Engage in discussions in our forum, where you can share your experiences and learn from others.
  8. Search for doctors and specialists through our easy-to-use search function.
  9. If you need any help or have questions, visit the Contact Us page to get in touch with us.

Follow these steps to make the most out of your therapy journey on our platform. We’re committed to providing you with the best tools and support for your needs.
