Welcome, Therapist!

Your personalized therapy dashboard.

Our platform allows you to monitor patient progress with comprehensive insights and detailed reports. Review session notes, track improvements, and identify areas that need more focus. As a therapist, your role is crucial in guiding patients on their journey to improved mental health and well-being. This dashboard is designed to provide you with the tools and resources you need to deliver exceptional care. Here, you can manage your appointments, review patient progress, and access therapeutic resources all in one place. We aim to make your experience as seamless as possible, allowing you to focus on what you do best—helping your patients achieve their goals. Maintaining confidentiality and privacy is our top priority. Communicate securely with your patients through our encrypted messaging system, ensuring that all conversations are protected. Feel confident knowing that your patient interactions are safe and secure.

Webpage Flow and Steps:

  1. Home Dashboard: Upon logging in, therapists land on the dashboard where they can access all essential tools.
  2. Chatbot Interaction: Engage with patients through the chatbot for quick consultations or therapy sessions.
  3. Discussion Forum: Participate in professional discussions, share knowledge, and collaborate with other therapists.
  4. Patient Sessions: Manage and monitor patient progress, schedule appointments, and review session notes.
  5. Search Doctors: Find and connect with specialized professionals or partners within the platform.
  6. Session Notes: Take and save notes during or after sessions to keep a record of patient progress.
  7. Logout and Navigation: Easily navigate back to the home page or log out of the session securely.
